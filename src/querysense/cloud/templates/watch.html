Connect a database to start watching

1

Start the watch daemon

querysense watch --dsn postgresql://localhost/mydb --interval 60
2

Configure alerts (optional)

querysense watch --dsn postgresql://localhost/mydb \ --slack-webhook https://hooks.slack.com/services/... \ --pagerduty-key your-routing-key
3

Tune sensitivity

querysense watch --threshold 3.0 --min-severity 50
